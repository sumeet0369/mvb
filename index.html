Accounts & Book-keeping Services

Accounts and Bookkeeping services ensure accurate and organized financial management for businesses of all sizes. We offer structured Chart of Accounts setup for tracking assets, liabilities, revenue, expenses, and equity. Our Accounting Services span financial, managerial, cost, tax, and nonprofit accounting to support insights and compliance. Accounts Receivable (AR) Management helps streamline invoicing, collections, and credit control. We manage the Invoice-to-Cash Process with efficient approval workflows and vendor reconciliation. Detailed Transaction Logging ensures audit-ready financial records.

  • GAAP-compliant financial reporting
  • Customizable reports and dashboards
  • Real-time financial insights and tracking

Financial Reporting & Analysis

Our Financial Reporting & Analysis services provide clear, data-driven insights to support strategic business decisions. We prepare accurate Financial Statements including Income Statements, Balance Sheets, and Cash Flow Statements. Through Variance & Ratio Analysis, we compare actuals against budgets and assess financial health. Trend & Segment Analysis highlights growth patterns and unit-wise performance. We also conduct Revenue, Cost & Cash Flow Analysis to improve profitability and liquidity management. Our Regulatory & Management Reporting ensures compliance with GAAP/IFRS and empowers leadership with actionable insights.

  • Real-time financial dashboards
  • Customized KPI tracking
  • Forecasting & budgeting support

Budgeting & Forecasting

Our Budgeting & Forecasting services help businesses plan effectively and make informed financial decisions. We handle Budget Preparation by aligning financial plans with historical data and future goals. Through Revenue & Expense Planning, we project income and allocate costs to maximize efficiency. Variance Analysis highlights discrepancies between actual and projected performance for corrective action. Our Financial Modeling simulates various business scenarios to support strategic planning and risk mitigation.

  • Scenario-based planning tools
  • Rolling forecasts for agility

Inventory Management

Our Inventory Management services ensure efficient control over stock to reduce costs and meet demand effectively. We offer Inventory Planning by forecasting demand and optimizing stock levels. Order Placement & Receiving includes managing procurement, supplier coordination, and quality checks upon delivery. With Inventory Tracking & Valuation, we utilize systems and methods like FIFO/LIFO for real-time tracking and accurate financial reporting. Disposal Management ensures proper handling of obsolete, expired, or damaged inventory to maintain warehouse efficiency and compliance.

  • Real-time inventory visibility
  • Automated reorder alerts
  • Integration with accounting systems

Payroll Management

Our Payroll Management services streamline employee compensation with precision and compliance. We handle Timekeeping & Payroll Calculation, accurately tracking hours worked and computing wages, bonuses, and deductions. Tax Withholding & Compliance ensures proper tax deductions and adherence to legal requirements. Payroll Distribution facilitates timely salary payments via direct deposit or checks. Additionally, Payroll Reconciliation matches payroll data with accounting records to maintain accuracy and audit readiness.

  • Automated payroll processing
  • Compliance with local tax laws
  • Confidential and secure data handling

Tax Services

Our Tax Services provide comprehensive support for individuals and businesses to ensure compliance and optimize tax outcomes. We handle Tax Preparation & Filing for federal and state returns accurately and on time. Through Tax Planning & Strategy, we proactively minimize tax liabilities. We maximize benefits by managing Deductions, Credits & Compliance within legal frameworks. Our team also offers Audit Support & Amendments to assist with audits and correct past filings. Additionally, we provide Consultation & Quarterly Tax Assessment for strategic guidance and estimated tax payments.

  • Up-to-date with latest tax laws
  • Personalized tax-saving strategies
  • Transparent and timely filing process

Accounts Receivable & Accounts Payable Management

We streamline your accounts receivable and accounts payable processes to ensure smooth cash flow and accurate financial records. From invoice-to-cash management to vendor payments, our team handles every step efficiently.

Accounts Receivable (AR): We manage invoicing, payment tracking, collections, credit control, and reporting to keep your cash inflow on track.

Accounts Payable (AP): We handle bill and invoice processing, payment approvals, vendor management, expense tracking, and timely payments — ensuring your financial operations run seamlessly.

  • End-to-end invoice processing and tracking
  • Timely vendor payments and reconciliation
  • Accurate cash flow forecasting and reporting
