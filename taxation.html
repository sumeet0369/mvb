Taxation Services

Tax Services

Tax Preparation:

Assist with the preparation and filing of Federal and all State’s tax returns for individuals and businesses.

Tax Planning:

Offer strategies to minimize tax liabilities through planning and proactive management.

Document Assembly:

Gather and organize necessary financial documents, including W-2s, 1099s, and other appropriate records.

Deductions and Credits:

Identify and apply eligible deductions and tax credits to reduce taxable income and maximize refunds.

Compliance Review:

Ensure tax returns comply with current federal and state tax laws and regulations.

Filing Decisions:

Stipulate various filing options, including e-filing, paper filing, and extension requests.

Audit Care:

Offer assistance and representation in case of IRS audits or inquiries.

Amendments and Corrections:

Prepare and file amended returns to correct errors or update information on previously filed tax returns.

Tax Liability Assessment:

Calculate estimated tax liabilities and provide guidance on making quarterly estimated tax payments.

Consultation and Intelligence:

Provide personalized tax advice and consulting services to address specific tax-related issues and planning needs.
